Jeremy (A.K.A. Indiana) Scones is Jones' cousin.

For the record, a scone is a dry English cake.
Therefore, Indiana Scones has a dry sense of humor.

His favorite joke is:
Q. "Why couldn't the tiger play cards in the Jungle?"

A. "'Cause there were too many cheatahs!"
